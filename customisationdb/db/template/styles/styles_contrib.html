Styles database

Welcome to phpBB.com's Styles Database. Here you will find downloadable styles to replace the default phpBB ones (subSilver and prosilver for phpBB 2.0.x and 3.0.x respectively), as well as add-ons such as rank images, avatars, and more. All styles in our database go through a rigorous validation process by the phpBB Styles Team; this is done in order to minimize the effort required when installing styles, and to ensure a certain level of overall quality.

If you have questions or comments about a specific style in the database, please post in the style's respective thread. If you have general questions about styling, please post in either the [2.0.x] Styles Development and Discussion forum or the [3.0.x] Styles Development and Discussion forum, dependent of course on the version you are using.

If you wish to design and submit your own style, please see our Styles Submissions Policy.
